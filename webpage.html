Arijith singh : A versatile singer

Arijit Singh: The Soulful Voice of Bollywood. Arijit Singh is one of the most popular and versatile singers in Bollywood, who has sung over 300 songs in various languages and genres.He started his musical journey at the age of three, learning classical music from his grandmother and later from three renowned gurus in West Bengal.He participated in the reality show Fame Gurukul in 2005, where he was eliminated in the sixth position, but got a chance to work with music director Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy as an assistant.He made his Bollywood debut in 2011 with the song "Phir Mohabbat" from Murder 2, which was composed by Mithoon and became a hit. He also sang four versions of "Raabta" from Agent Vinod, which showcased his versatility.Arijit Singh is a popular singer who won awards for songs like 'Tum Hi Ho' and 'Kabira' with composers like Pritam and A.R. Rahman.He is known for his soulful and expressive voice, which can convey a range of emotions and moods. Some of his popular songs are "Channa Mereya", "Hawayein", "Tujhe Kitna Chahne Lage", "Khairiyat", and "Deva Deva".
